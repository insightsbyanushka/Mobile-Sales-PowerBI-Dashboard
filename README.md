# ✏️Project Overview 
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze mobile sales data. The dashboard helps understand sales performance, customer behavior, payment methods, brand performance, and city-wise sales.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Compare brand-wise sales
- Identify top-performing cities
- Analyze customer ratings
- Study payment method distribution
- Track monthly and weekly sales trends

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX
- Data Modeling

---

## 📊 KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Transactions
- Average Sales
- Units Sold
- Brand Performance

---

## ✨ Dashboard Features

- Brand-wise Sales Analysis
- City-wise Sales Map
- Monthly Sales Filter
- Payment Method Distribution
- Customer Rating Analysis
- Weekly Sales Trend

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Apple generated the highest sales.
- UPI was the most used payment method.
- Major cities drove the majority of sales.
- Customer ratings were consistently positive.
- Business achieved ₹19M in total sales through 89 transactions.
